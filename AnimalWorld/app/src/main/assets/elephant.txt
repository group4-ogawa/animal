Elephants are mammals of the family Elephantidae and the largest existing land animals.They live in groups of about 10, and their young grow up protected by the group.
They eats about 150 kg of grass, leaves, fruits, and vegetables per day. Despite its huge size, they can run at about 40 km/h.
It is said that they speak in low frequency sounds that humans cannot hear, and their cries have a sound pressure of up to about 112 dB, which has been reported to reach up to 10 km away.
